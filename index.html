About Us

FIRST Robotics Competition Team 11362 is a student-led robotics team dedicated to innovation, teamwork, and STEM education. We design, build, and program industrial-sized robots to compete in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our Team

Mechanical

Designs and builds the robot's structure, drivetrain, and mechanisms.

Electrical

Wires and powers the robot, ensuring reliability and safety.

Programming

Develops autonomous and teleoperated code for competition.

Business & Media

Handles outreach, sponsorships, branding, and team media.

Our Robots

Each season, our team creates a new robot from the ground up to meet the challenge released by FIRST. From CAD design to on-field performance, our robots represent months of collaboration and problem-solving.

Outreach & Community

We believe in giving back. Team 11362 actively participates in STEM outreach events, mentors younger teams, and engages with our community to inspire the next generation of engineers.
